Feel free to pitch in via GitHub.

Possible nuances and fixes (may not be implemented below):

  • Use focusable="false" for IE to ensure decorative SVG is not focusable and no double focus for links.
  • Older AT/browser combinations may not announce aria-describedby.
  • Safari bug (now fixed): IMG elements with an SVG src are ignored by VO despite valid alt attribute
  • Voiceover support on iOS and OSX may be broken.
  • Force focus in IE using tabindex=0
